# System A0: Relational Architecture vs. Surveillance

## 1. The Core Thesis
Modern care-tech often relies on **surveillance** (cameras, microphones). This creates a "patient-observer" dynamic that erodes dignity. 

**System A0 replaces surveillance with Contextual Presence.**

## 2. Technical Philosophy: Privacy by Abstraction
We utilize the **Hex-Dust Protocol** to solve the privacy paradox.
* **Input:** Environmental sensors (IR, thermal, occupancy).
* **Processing:** Edge-AI filters out identifiable biometric data.
* **Output:** A stream of Hex color codes representing "emotional temperature".
* **Result:** The family knows the senior is safe without "watching" them.

## 3. The Shadow-Touch Layer
A0 implements a haptic bridge using thermoelectric elements.
* **Synchronous Presence:** When a family member interacts, the senior feels localized warmth or a "pulse".
* **Emotional Telemetry:** Creates a pre-verbal communication channel.

## 4. Integration Strategy
Project A0 is designed as a **Headless System**, compatible with Google Home (Matter) or specialized wearables.
